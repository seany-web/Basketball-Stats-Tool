BALANCE TEAMS FUNCTION:
_________________________

find out the maximum team size so teams are balanced
get maximum number of experienced players on each team
get maximum number of inexperienced players on each team

loop through clean player list
    find out if current player in list is experienced
    if team 1 has room in squad
        add 1 to team 1 squad size
        if player is not experienced AND there is room in squad
            put player in team 1
            add 1 to inexperienced players
        else if player is experienced and there is room in squad
            put player in team 1
            add 1 to experienced players
    else if team 1 is full but team 2 has room in squad
        add 1 to team 2 squad size
        if player is not experienced AND there is room in squad
            put player in team 2
            add 1 to inexperienced players
        else if player is experienced and there is room in squad
            put player in team 2
            add 1 to experienced players
    else
        add 1 to team 3 squad size
        if player is experienced
            put player in team 3
            add 1 to experienced players
        else
            put player in team 3
            add 1 to inexperienced players
